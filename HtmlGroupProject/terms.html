Tips For Safe Travel

×

Wash hands regularly

Avoid touching your own eyes, nose and mouth with unwashed hands, as viruses most frequently enter the body through these routes.

Practice good hygiene

If soap and water are not available to clean your hands, use a hand sanitizer which is at least 60% alcohol.

Cover up

Cover your mouth and nose when you cough, sneeze into your elbow and wear a facemask to protect your fellow travelers.

Have vaccinations up-to-date

While there is no vaccine for COVID-19, having all other essential vaccinations up-to-date will help you stay healthy and prevent your immune system from being compromised.

Maintain social distance

Social distance must be maintained at all times during travel and during interactions with people outside the group.

02. PRICING


- Activities will include additional fees where stipulated. Prices for all activities are for per adult or child. The price will be subject to revision in event of significant changes in exchange rates, tariff rates, taxes etc.
- All prices for trails and activities exclude GST and will be charged extra. Prices include CanCamper’s fee for planning and arranging itineraries, handling and operational charges.

- Price includes travel, stay (in Caravans only), Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, non-Alcoholic beverages on each day of the journey. Any other costs are excluded and will be charged extra at actuals.


03. BOOKING PROCESS


- Booking a Trail can be done electronically or by interacting with our office. The Guest is responsible for confirming the itinerary to CanCamper in writing by e-mail to enable CanCamper to make the required itinerary arrangements.
- CANCAMPER will extend provisional bookings subject to availability. Confirmed bookings will be made only on receipt of 100% payments. CANCAMPER reserves the right to release Provisional bookings.
- Upon confirmation the Guest’s passport nationality will be required, which information will be used solely for submission to law enforcement agencies and for determination of entry/activity fees as applicable - Reservations are confirmed on receipt of 100% payment 10 days prior to Trail start date, in advance including the GST amount. The final Tax invoice will be sent to the Guests by email within 48 hours of completion of the Trail - GST details of the Guest/s if any must be provided to CanCamper at the time of booking so that appropriate invoice reflecting the GST credit shall be raised on completion of the Trail - Confirmed Accommodation will be subject to the appropriate payment and Cancellation policy


04. EXCLUSIONS


- Unless expressly included, all and any cost (without limitation) of obtaining passports, visas, items of a personal nature such as drinks, laundry, telephone calls, communication and excess baggage charges shall be excluded from the Trail pricing.
