This was a group project and our project title is CAN CAMPER, The CanCamper is your private holiday home on wheels designed to travel safely and maintain social distance.
The vehicle is designed to carry upto 4 members of a family or close friends.
Our trips are highly personalised so that we could focus on creating a safe travel environment throughout the journey. The guests can literally control the cabin environment for safety from any external contamination.
